üöÄ What is Schemarama?

Schemarama provides ShEx (Shape Expressions) validation schemas for Schema.org structured data, tailored to the specific requirements of major search engines and platforms.


This project helps developers validate their structured data markup against platform-specific guidelines, ensuring rich results appear correctly across Google, Bing, Pinterest, and more.

  • JobPosting - Job listings and employment opportunities
  • Organization - Companies and institutions
  • Video - Video content with metadata
  • Movie - Film information and reviews
  • Course - Educational courses and programs
  • SoftwareApplication - Apps and software
  • FAQ/QA Pages - Question and answer content
  • Review - Product and service reviews

üìñ Schema Organization

Schemas are organized in a hierarchical structure:

  • demo/validation/shex/shapes/ - Base Schema.org type definitions
  • demo/validation/shex/specific/Google/ - Google-specific requirements
  • demo/validation/shex/specific/Pinterest/ - Pinterest Rich Pins
  • demo/validation/shex/specific/Bing/ - Bing requirements
